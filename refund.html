Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Here is how our refund process works.

Last Updated: March 2026

Section 01

Refund Eligibility

BookVerse offers refunds under the following circumstances:

  • Damaged Books — If you receive a book that is physically damaged, torn, has missing pages, or has a defective binding
  • Wrong Items — If you receive a book that is different from what you ordered
  • Missing Items — If one or more items from your order are missing from the delivered package

Refunds are subject to verification by our team. We may request photographic evidence of the issue before processing your refund.

Section 02

Reporting Timeframe

You must report any issue with your order within 48 hours of delivery. Reports received after this window may not be eligible for a refund. Please include clear photographs of the damaged, wrong, or missing items along with your order details.

The 48-hour window begins from the time of delivery as recorded by our shipping partner. We recommend inspecting your order promptly upon receipt.

Section 03

Refund Process

Once you report an issue, the refund process follows these steps:

1

Submit Your Request

Email us at hello@bookverse.in with your order ID, description of the issue, and clear photographs.

2

Inspection & Review

Our team will review your request and the submitted evidence within 1-2 business days.

3

Approval & Notification

You will receive an email confirming whether your refund has been approved or if additional information is needed.

4

Refund Processed

Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method or as store credit.

Section 04

Refund Methods

Refunds will be processed through one of the following methods:

  • Original Payment Method — If you paid via Razorpay (UPI, credit/debit card, net banking), the refund will be credited back to the same payment method. Processing time may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
  • Store Credit — You may opt to receive the refund as BookVerse store credit, which can be used for future purchases. Store credits do not expire.

Please note that while we process refunds within 5-7 business days, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the amount to reflect in your bank account, depending on your financial institution.

Section 05

Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Books that have been read, used, highlighted, annotated, or show signs of handling beyond initial inspection
  • Book bundles or sets where the seal or packaging has been opened (unless the item inside is damaged or wrong)
  • Orders where the correct items have been delivered as described on the website
  • Items reported after the 48-hour reporting window has elapsed
  • Refund requests without sufficient photographic evidence when requested
Section 06

Damaged in Transit

If your book arrives damaged due to mishandling during transit, BookVerse will offer you either:

  • Full Replacement — We will ship a new copy of the same book at no additional cost, subject to availability
  • Full Refund — If the book is out of stock, we will process a complete refund to your original payment method

For transit damage claims, please photograph the packaging as well as the damaged book before disposing of any materials, as this may be needed for our claim with the courier service.

Section 07

Partial Orders

If you placed a multi-item order and one or more items are missing from the delivered package:

  • You will receive a refund for the value of the missing item(s) only
  • If the missing items are available in stock, we may offer to ship them separately at no additional cost
  • Shipping charges will be recalculated — if the remaining delivered items fall below the free delivery threshold of ₹399, the original shipping rate will still be honoured
Section 08

COD Order Refunds

For orders placed via Cash on Delivery (COD), refunds will be processed through UPI or direct bank transfer. You will be asked to provide your UPI ID or bank account details (account number, IFSC code) so that we can initiate the transfer. COD refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after approval.

Section 09

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund request, please send an email with the following details:

Email: hello@bookverse.in

Include in your email:
• Your Order ID
• Description of the issue (damaged / wrong item / missing item)
• Clear photographs showing the problem
• Photograph of the packaging (for transit damage claims)
• Your preferred refund method (original payment / store credit)

Our support team aims to acknowledge all refund requests within 24 hours of receipt.
