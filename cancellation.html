Cancellation Policy

Need to cancel an order? Here is everything you need to know about our cancellation process.

Last Updated: March 2026

Section 01

Before Shipment

You may cancel your order free of charge within 12 hours of placing it, provided the order has not yet been shipped. Cancellations within this window are processed immediately with no questions asked.

To check whether your order has been shipped, refer to the order confirmation email or contact us at hello@bookverse.in. If the order status shows "Processing" or "Confirmed," it can still be cancelled. If it shows "Shipped" or "In Transit," cancellation is no longer possible.

Section 02

After Shipment

Once your order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled. At this stage, the package is already in transit with our courier partner, and we are unable to recall it.

If you no longer wish to keep the order after it has been shipped, you may:

  • Accept the delivery and then follow our Refund Policy if the item qualifies for a refund (e.g., damaged or wrong item)
  • Refuse the delivery at the doorstep — however, please note that refunds for refused shipments may take longer to process and return shipping costs may apply

We recommend accepting the delivery and contacting us if you have any issues with the order.

Section 03

How to Cancel

To cancel your order, send an email with the following details:

Email: hello@bookverse.in

Include in your email:
• Your Order ID
• Reason for cancellation (optional but helpful)
• Your registered email address or phone number

Our team will process your cancellation request and send you a confirmation email. If the order has already been shipped, we will inform you accordingly and suggest alternative options.

Section 04

Refund on Cancellation

When a cancellation is approved:

Immediate

Cancellation Confirmed

You will receive an email confirming that your order has been successfully cancelled.

1-2 Business Days

Refund Initiated

The full refund is initiated to your original payment method (Razorpay — UPI, card, or net banking).

3-5 Business Days

Refund Credited

The refund amount is credited to your account. Bank processing times may vary.

Cancellation refunds are always for the full order amount, including any shipping charges you may have paid.

Section 05

COD Cancellation

Cash on Delivery Orders

If you placed a COD order and cancel it before shipment, no charges will be applied. Since no payment has been collected, no refund is necessary.

If a COD order is cancelled after the payment has been collected at delivery (e.g., due to a return), the refund will be processed via UPI or bank transfer. You will be asked to provide your UPI ID or bank details for the transfer.

Section 06

Partial Cancellation

If you placed a multi-item order and wish to cancel only some of the items:

  • Partial cancellation is allowed for orders that have not yet been shipped
  • You will receive a refund for the cancelled item(s) only
  • The remaining items will be shipped as scheduled
  • Shipping charges will be recalculated — if the remaining items fall below the free delivery threshold of ₹399, standard shipping charges may apply
  • If the order has already been packed or shipped, partial cancellation may not be possible

To request a partial cancellation, email us at hello@bookverse.in with your Order ID and the specific items you wish to cancel.

Section 07

Repeated Cancellations

We understand that plans change. However, frequent or repeated cancellations may impact our operations and logistics. If an account shows a pattern of excessive cancellations, BookVerse reserves the right to:

  • Flag the account for review
  • Temporarily restrict Cash on Delivery (COD) as a payment option for the account
  • In extreme cases, limit the ability to place new orders until the matter is reviewed

We aim to be fair and understanding, and such measures will only be taken in cases of clear misuse. If you believe your account has been flagged in error, please contact us and we will review the situation.

Section 08

Contact Information

Our team is available to assist you Monday through Saturday, and we aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
